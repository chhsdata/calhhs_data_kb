# G: State and County Population Projections

View the “[CalHHS Data De-Identification Guidelines Reference Dataset](https://data.chhs.ca.gov/dataset/chhs-ddg)” resource on the California Health & Human Services Agency’s Open Data Portal.

This DDG resource:

* Provides California population estimates related to the race and ethnicity composition at the county level based on data from the 2020 U.S. Census.
* Includes more detailed race and ethnicity breakdowns than provided in prior DDG versions.
