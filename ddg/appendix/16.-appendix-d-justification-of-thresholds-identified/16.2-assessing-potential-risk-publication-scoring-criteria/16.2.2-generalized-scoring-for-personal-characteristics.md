# 16.2.2 Generalized Scoring for Personal Characteristics

Scores for all personal characteristics (e.g., age, race/ethnicity, language spoken) have been determined based on thresholds derived from the statewide population sizes as below and the [CalHHS DDG Edition 1.0](#user-content-fn-1)[^1] age range scores. &#x20;
